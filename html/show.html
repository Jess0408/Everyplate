    After the beef is cooked on medium-high heat, slowly fry. When the meat is about 80% cooked, add the seasoning powder together and continue to stir fry until it is fully cooked. After fully cooked, add a little warm water, and boil the water after 10 minutes to dry, about a little water foam over the meat. Bring the water to a boil and simmer on medium heat for six or seven minutes.
